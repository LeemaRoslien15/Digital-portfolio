Types of Cyber Crimes

  • Phishing: Fake emails to steal personal data.
  • Ransomware: Malware that locks files for ransom.
  • Identity Theft: Using someone’s identity without permission.
  • Cyberbullying: Online harassment and abuse.

Case Studies

Example 1: In 2022, a phishing scam targeted students using fake portals to steal credentials.

Example 2: A ransomware attack locked hospital data until a Bitcoin ransom was paid.

Safety Tips

  • Don't share OTPs or passwords.
  • Use strong, unique passwords.
  • Install and update antivirus software.
  • Don’t click unknown links or download random files.
